Announcements
Announcements pertaining to the MSB Sangha are displayed and archived on this webpage. Please check this page on a regular basis to stay up-to-date on the most current Sangha news.

Bulletin Board
This page is available for the MSB Sangha and the larger Tibetan Buddhist community to post general messages related to topics such as events, car pooling, help wanted, items wanted, etc. Please contact the MSB Secretary if you have any postings for this page.

Phüntsok Chöling Calendar
The current monthly calendar of practice events including tsok dates, group sitting practice schedule, assigned umdzes, and torma making dates are displayed on this page.

Pema Osel Calendar
The current monthly calendar of practice events including tsok dates, group sitting practice schedule, assigned umdzes, and torma making dates are displayed on this page.

Prayer Requests
This webpage includes the names of close ones connected to MSB Sangha members who are ill or recently deceased. The Sangha is asked to recite Refuge Prayers for the benefit of these loved ones.

Service
Visit this page to view the most current listing of service related positions held by Sangha members.

Donations Online
Pay your dues online with a credit card. The recommended monthly donation is $50, however you can enter any amount you wish.
