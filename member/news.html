Personal Letter from Kongtrul Rinpoche

Dear Noble Sangha,

His Holiness Penor Rinpoche passed peacefully into nirvana last night at his monastery near Mysore, India.

Kongtrul Rinpoche has been present here in South India and has the following personal message to the whole Sangha:

"At this crucial time it is important to come together as a Sangha and
individually, to practice the Guru Yoga wholeheartedly. Please mingle your
mind with the the guru and make deep and pure aspirations to be connected to
him as we have been in this life, throughout all our future lives,
continuously.

In the post meditation we should make lamp offerings and contribute to the
on going offerings and various pujas that the monastery will be conducting
from this point forward. These pujas and offerings are made in order to
fulfill all of His Holiness Penor Rinpoche's aspirations, both now and far
into the future.

We are incredibly fortunate to have met such a master with such great and
profound realization and heart for the Buddhadharma. His tireless endeavor
in spreading the Dharma and caring for beings is exemplary in how a
bodhisattva has truly taken birth in this world.

Warmest Wishes to you all,

Rinpoche"
