11/8/09
The Visit 2010- Accommodations Info for Vermont

Pema Osel Do Ngak Choling has arranged with Gove Hill Retreat, Thetford, VT to offer housing during (as well as before/after for volunteering if enough people) the visit of Khyentse Yangsi Rinpoche in August 2010.

Packages will for the most part include bed, linens, and breakfast. Carpooling and shuttling will be available to/from Pema Osel. There are a few pricing options. Please visit the Gove Hill Retreat website to take a peek. However, for pricing/reservations and/or general questions, (and, at the request of Gove Hill), please contact Marcia at drakemarcia4@gmail.com for details and/or reservations.

This information will be announced to general public via The Visit/Pema Osel website shortly and later into the year.
